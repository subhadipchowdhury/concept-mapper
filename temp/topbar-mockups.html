Quick comparison
A Segmented switcher Clean, predictable. Minimal change from current structure. Segmented pill is clearer than a flat "active" button. Best if you want a safe, polished upgrade.
B Centered logo Strong identity. Feels like a branded product. Slightly more complex layout. Role chip with status dot gives a nice "mode indicator" feel.
C Minimal ribbon Most canvas space. Breadcrumb-as-drawer-trigger is clever. Overflow menu hides complexity. Best for power users / mobile.
D Frosted glass Most visual interest. Gradient accent line is subtle but distinctive. Blue-tinted breadcrumb ties UI to the accent colour. Easiest to mix with A.
